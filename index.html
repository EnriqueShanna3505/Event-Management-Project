About this Project

Welcome to A Realtime Interactive Event Management Chatbot! This project was developed as part of my final year university project, aimed at revolutionizing how people discover events. By leveraging advanced chatbot technology, I created a system that makes discovering events effortless by matching users with experiences that align with their interests. Whether you're passionate about music, technology, fitness, or networking, this intelligent chatbot ensures you never miss out on the best opportunities. The project was designed to handle the search process seamlessly, allowing users to focus on creating unforgettable memories. It represents the culmination of my academic journey, combining technical skills, creativity, and problem-solving to deliver a practical and innovative solution!
